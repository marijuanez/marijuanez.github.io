The Story
In fact, he was forced by his father to study Graphic Arts. He went to work on it without having finished the professional training cycle. Displaying a strong interest and skills in graphic design and photography. In what has become a calm and peaceful space, he organizes himself an unusual and secluded universe where he acquires photographs, drawings, paintings, collages and other artistic creations.

Career
After having lived in Bilbao, Tenerife, London, Madrid and finally in Munich, sometimes without being able to enjoy the passion for painting for reasons of space or work that as a Webmaster he has been developing in his life.
